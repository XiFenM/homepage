I'm not playing it for the tower defense. I'm playing it because **it's willing to tell a quieter, less satisfying story**.

## Pace

The game gives me about three things to do per week. That restraint makes it a **small fixed window** in my life — it won't bloat, won't demand my full attention.

## Art

There's an "author present" quality to its art. Unlike most mobile games, I can feel the artist thinking — why this stroke starts here, why that area was left empty.
