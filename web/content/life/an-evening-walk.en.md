I took my headphones off and realized how loud the city actually is. Then, after a few minutes, I noticed the noise has a rhythm to it — distant traffic, gravel underfoot, a small dog grumbling on someone's balcony.

By the time I reached the river I caught the last five minutes of sunset. The clouds were low; the color was the orange of old photographs.

## Nothing in particular

On the way home I bought a paper bag of roasted chestnuts and burned a finger peeling one. Nothing in particular — just wanted to write it down.
