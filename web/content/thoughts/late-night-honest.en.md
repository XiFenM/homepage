After 1 AM, the self-censoring muscle gets tired first. What I write at that hour is often not *good*, but it's usually **closer to true**.

## What I've been turning over

- How many things have I dressed up as "I like this," when really I just don't want to admit they **don't matter to me as much anymore**?
- Have I been using "busy" as an excuse not to have a conversation with myself?
- The people who keep showing up in my head — do they know?

No answers. Just writing it down.
